
# Task 1 - Sales Performance Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze retail sales data and create an interactive dashboard to identify sales trends, profit performance, and business insights.

## Dataset
- Dataset: Sample Superstore Dataset
- File Used: Sample - Superstore.csv

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Sales

## Visualizations

### Sales by Category
Analyzes sales performance across different product categories.

### Sales by Region
Shows sales distribution across regions.

### Profit by Category
Identifies the most profitable product categories.

### Sales Trend Over Time
Tracks sales performance across different periods.

### Sales by Segment
Compares sales among customer segments.

## Filters (Slicers)
- Region
- Category
- Segment

## Key Insights

- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- Some categories showed high sales but lower profit margins.
- Regional performance varied significantly across markets.
- Consumer segment contributed a major share of sales.

## Recommendations

1. Focus on high-performing product categories.
2. Improve profitability in low-margin categories.
3. Increase marketing efforts in high-growth regions.
4. Monitor sales trends regularly for better forecasting.
