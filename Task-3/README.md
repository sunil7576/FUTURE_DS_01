# Task 3 - Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze marketing campaign data to identify conversion performance, customer behavior patterns, and opportunities to improve lead-to-customer conversion rates.

## Dataset

- Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository
- File Used: bank-full.csv

## Key Insights

### Education Analysis
- Customers with secondary and tertiary education contributed the highest conversions.
- Unknown education category showed the lowest conversion count.

### Contact Analysis
- Cellular contact method generated the highest number of successful conversions.
- Telephone and unknown contact methods performed significantly lower.

### Job Analysis
- Management and technician professions showed higher conversion counts.
- Retired and services categories had comparatively lower conversions.

### Monthly Performance
- Marketing campaigns performed best during May and August.
- Conversion counts were lowest in January and December.

## Recommendations

1. Increase marketing efforts through cellular contact channels.
2. Focus campaigns on high-performing customer professions.
3. Allocate more budget to months with historically higher conversions.
4. Create personalized campaigns based on education level and customer profile.

## Conclusion
The dashboard provides a clear view of customer conversion behavior and marketing campaign effectiveness. Businesses can use these insights to optimize campaign strategies and improve overall conversion performance.
